Frequently Asked

Questions We
Hear Every Day

How do I enroll in a course at Ravindra IAS?

You can enroll by filling out the contact form on this page, calling our helpline, or visiting our Delhi centre directly. Our counsellor will guide you through the right program based on your background, attempt number, and goals. Enrollment for new batches opens every quarter.

What is the duration of each course?

Our Foundation Program runs for 24 months. Prelims Intensive is 6 months, Mains Answer Writing is 8 months, Interview Guidance is 2 months, and Optional Subject programs are 5–7 months depending on the subject. All programs have clearly defined milestones and deliverables.

Are classes available online as well as offline?

Yes! We offer full hybrid learning — all classes are conducted live at our Delhi centre AND simultaneously streamed online. Recorded sessions are available for 90 days. Students from any part of India can access the complete program without relocating.

Do you provide mentorship for repeat (second/third) attempt aspirants?

Absolutely. In fact, many of our toppers cracked UPSC on their second or third attempt with us. We have a specific program for re-aspirants focused on identifying gaps, rebuilding strategy, and maximising efficiency. Our counsellors personally design a study plan for each re-attempting student.

Is there a free trial or demo class available?

Yes! We offer free demo classes for all major programs. You can attend one live session and access two weeks of study material before committing to enrollment. Contact our team to schedule your free demo and get a complimentary UPSC Starter Kit worth ₹2,000.

What makes Ravindra IAS different from other coaching institutes?

Three things set us apart: personalized mentorship (every student gets a dedicated guide), rigorous answer writing focus (most students fail Mains — we fix that), and our student-first culture. We maintain small batches of 60 max to ensure quality, and our founder personally reviews performance reports monthly.
